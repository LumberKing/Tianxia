The graphical submods all require one or more DLCs to work. Make sure you have the relevant DLCs active before activating the associated submods.

Tianxia: Ainu Portraits -- requires Turkish portraits
Tianxia: Chinese, Khmer, & Tibetan Portraits -- requires Jade Dragon
Tianxia: Japanese & Korean Portraits -- requires Mongol portraits
Tianxia: Khitan Portraits -- requires Jade Dragon and Turkish portraits
Tianxia: Khitan Unit Pack -- requires Byzantine, Turkish, and Mongol units
Tianxia: Late Western Clothing Pack -- requires The Republic
Tianxia: Tocharian Portraits -- requires Russian and Persian portraits

Note: Some of the folders and the names of the mod may not match as the general portrait mods are named after the DLC required.
